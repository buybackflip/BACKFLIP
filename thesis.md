# the thesis

## attention is finite. backflips are infinite.

every day, people land backflips.
on beaches, in gyms, in parking lots, at weddings.
and every day, those backflips get filmed and shared.

$BACKFLIP attaches itself to that stream.

---

## why backflips

not because they're rare — because they're *reliably* viral.

a good backflip post gets:
- comments from people who can't believe it
- shares from people who want their friends to see it
- saves from people who watch it six times

that's the engagement stack. and we live inside it.

---

## the play

meme coins need a hook.
ours is physical, visual, universal.

you don't need to speak english to appreciate a backflip.
you don't need to know what crypto is.
you just need to watch it and feel something.

that feeling is the on-ramp.

---

## what we are not

- we are not a utility token
- we are not a DAO (yet)
- we are not asking you to trust us

we are asking you to watch the flip.
and then decide.
